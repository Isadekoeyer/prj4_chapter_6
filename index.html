Vision

In a world where people tend to live on their own little planet, it is important to go on adventures outside of your comfort zone, into the universe. Only then we can expand our imagination and discover our true courage.

Our brand wants to encourage young gamers to start using their gaming skills and courage in real life! By building a supportive community we hope to encourge eachother and explore the wonders of the world.

Everyone ages 18-25 is welcome to join our community nights on Wednesday 20:00, Friday 21:30 and Saturday 21:30. You are free to join and leave whenever you like. Community nights are monitored by us. Things like racism, sexism and other disrespectful behavior will be banned.
